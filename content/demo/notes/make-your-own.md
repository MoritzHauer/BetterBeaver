# Build your own

Everything in this app — including this tutorial — is just Topics, Lessons,
and Units full of items, tasks, and notes. Nothing here is special-cased.

To make your own:

- Sign in, then tap the ✎ pencil on any Topic, Lesson, or Unit screen.
- Add items (words, facts, sentences) and tasks (quizzes on them).
- Group a handful into a Unit, a few Units into a Lesson, a few Lessons
  into a Topic.
- Edits autosave as a draft on your device. Nothing is public until you
  hit Publish.

Same ladder you just climbed — now you build with it.
